Coffee Export Division

Ethiopia's Finest Coffee, From Origin to the World

Himma Group specializes in the export of premium Ethiopian green coffee—both commercial and specialty grades.

Our coffee is sourced directly from Ethiopia's renowned coffee-growing regions, including Guji, Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, Limu, Jimma, and Harar. Each origin offers the unique flavors, aromas, and cup quality that make Ethiopian coffee world-famous.

Our washing station in Guji, Uraga gives us full control over cherry selection, processing, drying, and grading so every shipment is consistent and traceable.

About Himma Group

Himma Group is a diversified Ethiopian company operating in the coffee export and food supply industries. Founded on the principles of integrity, innovation, and excellence, we deliver premium products that represent the best of Ethiopia.
